Temple Entrance

Sandstone pillars flank the gaping entrance to the shrine. Carvings in languages long forgotten adorn the linings. The darkness of the inside absorbs all light entering it like a black hole. You check your notes again. The inscription on the entrance, ꗈ ꖖ ꕆ, matches the one in your notes.
Translation: Do Not Enter.
